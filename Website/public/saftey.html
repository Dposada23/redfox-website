SAFETY STATEMENT

Red Fox Contractor is dedicated to providing a safe workplace. We work hard in order to assure that every employee prioritizes safety. Supervisors conduct daily “toolbox meetings” for each individual to serve as both basic training and a reminder that safe behaviors are mandated. We believe that successful projects should be implemented without any incidents, and that everyone has a responsibility to guarantee that it transpires.

In addition to providing thorough safety training for our employees, we emphasize the significance of staying safe for our loved ones, colleagues, general well-being, and interests. We sustain a proactive safety culture while strengthening our overall safety performance by promoting superb safety initiatives and recognizing individuals who make them.
